## Goal

Make mobile use the relay as its sole ACP communication path in both text and voice mode. Two changes: (1) mobile sends typed text as `session/prompt` via relay in both modes (removes `text_message` on ganglia-events), and (2) mobile's `RelayChatService` subscribes to the `acp` topic in both modes (not just text mode). This merges the original T30.03 and T30.04 from the EPIC.

**Two-mode design (decided in planning):**

