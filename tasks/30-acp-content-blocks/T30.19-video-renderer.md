# Task 30.19: Video Renderer

**Epic:** 30 — ACP Content Blocks
**Status:** [ ]
**Depends on:** T30.07, T30.08
**Blocks:** none

## Goal

Build a renderer for video content arriving as ACP content blocks.

## Context

Video could arrive as embedded resources or resource links with video MIME types (`video/mp4`, etc.). Rendering requires a video player package.

## Deferred

**Why deferred:** No ACP agent currently emits video content. Video files are also large, making embedded delivery impractical without streaming infrastructure.

**Revisit when:** ACP agents emit video content or the resource delivery infrastructure (Epic 31) supports streaming.

## Relates to

- T30.07: RendererRegistry (would register for `video/*`)
- Epic 31: Resource Delivery (streaming delivery for large media)

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Video content renders as an inline player or metadata card with play action
- [ ] Registered in RendererRegistry for `video/*`
